    What are cookies?

    Cookies are files that are placed on a user's device that the user accessed the site when they visit the site. When you re-visit the site, the information collected and obtained through a cookie from a previous visit may be read.

    Cookies on the site do not allow the identification of an individual and cannot identify the specific user (first and last name or other personal information).

    Through cookies, the provider obtains information about how the site is used, in particular the frequency of visits to the site, the most read content, the average visit time, etc. (web analytics).
